Why Make a Reservation?

The Lord has commanded us to be prepared in all things. Temple Inn & Suites helps temple patrons to be prepared for their time in the temple by removing worries about accommodations, child care, and much more. We seek to promote a spirit of peace and reverence so that patrons will be in the right mindset before entering the House of the Lord. We believe that by preparing oneself mentally and physically, members of the church will find their visits to the temple more inspiring and enriching. The church has provided additional aidsfor use in preparing to enter the temple.
